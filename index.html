How It Works

Every 4 weeks we will broadcast the lottery from our youtube page (link at the top of page).

We will spin the first 3 wheels to select our Bad Boy of the Quarter.

Afterwards we will select the NFL Man of the Quarter.



Player Pool

The second wheel will generate a number which corresponds to a player on the 53 man roster. These are listed from 1 to 53 alphabetically by last name.

Bad Boy of the Quarter

Any player chosen in the lottery must be removed from all positions in the depth chart during suspension.



NFL Man of the Quarter

The first 2 wheels will be spun to select the winner.
The MOTQ winner will receive a 1pt xp boost to an attiribute of their choice.



Protected Players

Coaches may protect two players per season from suspension.These will be submitted at the beginning of each season and can only be modified due to a player no longer being on the team or injured.



*Appeals*

Coaches may appeal any suspension given by requesting to have the appeal wheel spun which has 2 options:



Option 1: Reversed: Your suspension number is halved. (If an odd number, it will be rounded up. i.e. 7 will be 4)

Option 2: Upheld: You will receive the selected number plus 2 games.
